I am a first-year EE PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Shuran Song. My research interest lies in robotics and embodied AI. Previously, I obtained a B.E. in automation engineering and a B.A. in product design from Tsinghua University. During my undergrad, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Leonidas Guibas at Stanford and Prof. Li Yi at Tsinghua University.
